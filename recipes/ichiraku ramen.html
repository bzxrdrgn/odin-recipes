Ichiraku ramen

Description

This is Naruto Uzumaki's favourite food

Ingridients

  • Broth 1 cup
  • Thinly cut pork meat
  • Egg 1
  • Noodles - 1 pack

Steps

  1. Boil broth
  2. Slightly fry the pork meat
  3. Start boiling broth in water
  4. Boil egg
  5. Put noodles
  6. Pour broth
  7. Add meat
  8. Add egg
  9. Enjoy!
